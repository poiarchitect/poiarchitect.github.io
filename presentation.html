PoIP – Project Overview

Where Proof Meets Impact. Built for Transparency, Purpose & Global Good.

Core Concept

The Proof of Impact Protocol (PoIP) is a decentralized funding system that allows users to stake $POI, grow a transparent treasury, and fund verified impact bounties around the world — in areas like aid, innovation, and climate repair. All funds are traceable. All rewards are symbolic. All impact is real.

Staking

Users stake $POI to help grow the PoIP treasury. Their funds are routed into a unified, low-risk DeFi yield pool. 100% of the original $POI is returned after unstaking. As a reward, stakers receive $GROW — a symbolic token with no financial promise but social significance in the PoIP ecosystem.

Treasury Logic

The treasury operates using smart contracts with zero human custody. Yield from pooled staking and reserve capital is split 50/50: half reinvested, half allocated to verified impact bounties. All fund flows are transparent, automated, and designed for long-term sustainability. The treasury enters dual-yield mode after £50M is secured.

AI + DAO Governance

The protocol uses an AI advisory layer to autonomously source, filter, and propose bounties from trusted NGO feeds. A DAO of $POI holders votes on approvals, treasury updates, and long-term protocol evolution. No centralized control. All decisions are publicly verifiable.

Symbolic Rewards

$GROW tokens are issued to stakers as a sign of contribution — not as profit. They represent alignment with real-world good and may unlock privileges in the future DAO (e.g. voting multipliers, social badges, or access roles). PoIP deliberately avoids speculative framing.

Transparency

All staking, treasury movements, and bounty disbursements are visible in real-time. Every funded bounty links to a public proof page. This ensures trust, encourages community validation, and builds a fully auditable history of impact.
