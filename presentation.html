Proof of Impact Protocol

Decentralized funding for real-world impact

Overview

The Proof of Impact Protocol (PoIP) is a blockchain-based system that allows users to stake tokens to grow a community treasury. This treasury funds verified impact projects in aid, innovation, and environmental recovery — transparently, on-chain.

Core Features

  • ✔️ Stake $POI to grow the treasury
  • ✔️ Earn symbolic $GROW tokens for staking
  • ✔️ Impact bounties funded from yield only
  • ✔️ Fully decentralized, DAO-governed system
