Proof of Impact Protocol (PoIP)

A trustless system for funding real-world impact — transparently, verifiably, and at scale.

Pitch Summary

Problem:
Billions in global aid are lost to inefficiencies, fraud, or bureaucracy. Donors lack transparency, and recipients struggle to access funds directly and verifiably.

Solution:
PoIP is a decentralized public-good protocol that turns crypto staking into verifiable real-world impact. Users stake tokens into a pooled treasury. The yield is used to fund AI-verified bounties (e.g. laptops for schools, clean water, medical supplies). Proof of delivery is posted publicly. Stakers earn symbolic $GROW tokens as reputation rewards.

How It Works:
• Stake $POI → treasury grows
• AI selects high-impact bounties
• Yield funds the solution directly
• On-chain proof confirms completion
• Stakers receive symbolic $GROW tokens

Why It’s Different:
• 100% transparent and auditable
• AI-powered, DAO-guided coordination
• No middlemen or speculation
• Only verified bounties are funded
• Built with sustainability and scale in mind
