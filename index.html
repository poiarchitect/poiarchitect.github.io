What is PoIP?

The Proof of Impact Protocol (PoIP) is an autonomous, non-profit blockchain system that funds verifiable global impact using treasury yield, not speculation or donations. It uses:

  • A low-risk DeFi treasury with transparent yield strategy
  • $POI staking to bootstrap reserves and enable participation
  • $GROW as a symbolic, non-tradable reward token with a dedicated voting page
  • On-chain bounty funding with AI-flagged proof verification
  • No DAO, no profit motive, and no custodial human control

The system is designed to last decades — immune to failure, speculation, and governance risk. It’s regenerative by default, and mathematically governed from day one.
