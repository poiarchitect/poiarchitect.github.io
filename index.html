The PoIP Foundation

The Proof of Impact Foundation is a UK-registered nonprofit that supports the growth and adoption of the PoIP protocol. It helps onboard NGOs, supports infrastructure, and communicates progress. The Foundation holds no $POI or $GROW, cannot access funds, and will phase into an advisory role once the protocol is fully autonomous.
