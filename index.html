What is PoIP?

The Proof of Impact Protocol (PoIP) is a regenerative funding system that converts treasury yield into real-world humanitarian, environmental, and innovation outcomes. PoIP uses symbolic rewards, conservative DeFi strategies, and automated proof verification to bypass extractive systems and redirect capital where it matters most.

Foundation Role

The Proof of Impact Foundation is a nonprofit support entity responsible for coordinating early infrastructure, AI model maintenance, NGO onboarding, and user education. It does not control funds, cannot execute contracts, and is designed to phase out once the system reaches treasury autonomy.

About $GROW

$GROW is a symbolic, non-transferable reward token granted to participants who stake $POI to grow the treasury. It carries no financial value, but may be used to vote on select bounty decisions on a dedicated impact participation page.
