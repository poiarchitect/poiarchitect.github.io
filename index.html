How It Works

PoIP uses low-risk DeFi strategies to generate yield from its treasury and staked assets. All funds go directly to proven bounties submitted by verified NGOs. $GROW tokens are awarded to stakers as symbolic proof of participation and can be used to vote on selected impact bounties.

Protocol Philosophy

PoIP is non-custodial, permissionless, and audit-friendly. It does not speculate, hold control mechanisms, or rely on human intermediaries to verify impact. Every transaction is documented, on-chain, and visible in real time.
