What is PoIP?

PoIP is a decentralized protocol that lets users stake $POI to grow a transparent treasury. Yield is used to fund real-world bounties — verified impact in aid, innovation, and climate recovery. Stakers receive $GROW as a symbolic reward for their support.

Why PoIP Matters

This is more than a protocol — it's a movement. Join a global system that verifies where funds go, how they’re used, and lets you contribute directly to real-world change through a transparent, smart-contract-controlled treasury.

The PoIP Foundation

The Proof of Impact Foundation is a UK-registered nonprofit that supports the growth and adoption of the PoIP protocol. It plays a coordination role — helping onboard NGOs, securing grant support, and managing public infrastructure like the website and documentation.

Importantly, the Foundation holds no $POI or $GROW tokens, cannot access treasury funds, and does not control protocol governance. All on-chain logic is fully decentralized and managed by smart contracts and the PoIP DAO.

Over time, as the protocol becomes fully autonomous, the Foundation will phase into an advisory role — supporting AI infrastructure and public transparency without interfering in decision-making.
