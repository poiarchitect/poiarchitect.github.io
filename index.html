Symbolic Rewards & Participation

Stakers earn $GROW, a non-transferable, symbolic reward token tied to reputation and community participation. $GROW enables voting on specific impact bounties via a dedicated page and does not hold financial value.

Impact Over Promises

PoIP only releases funds to verified NGOs with proof of delivery. All funded bounties must show evidence ‚Äî from images to satellite and sensor data ‚Äî before being recognized. This ensures every pound supports **real**, **visible**, **audited** action.

Foundation Support Model

The protocol is stewarded by the Proof of Impact Foundation, a non-profit entity that supports early operations, technical upkeep, and AI monitoring systems. The Foundation holds no access to funds or tokens and will phase out as protocol automation scales.
