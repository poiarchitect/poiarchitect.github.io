About the Protocol

PoIP is a fully autonomous protocol that allocates treasury yield to verified humanitarian, environmental, and scientific bounties — without human governance or speculation. The protocol is powered by:

  • A conservative yield-generating treasury
  • A staking system using the $POI token
  • A symbolic reputation token called $GROW
  • An AI advisory layer with smart contract fail-safes
  • On-chain proof submission and NGO verification logic

The protocol cannot be paused, changed, or owned. It is governed entirely by smart contracts and oracle-fed proof systems. The Foundation exists only to support early infrastructure and public transparency.

What is $POI?

$POI is the fixed-supply governance-aligned token of the protocol. It is used for staking, treasury bootstrapping, and potential future DEX visibility. It is not a reward-bearing or speculative asset. Most supply is treasury-held. A small portion may be used to bootstrap liquidity and build yield capacity.

What is $GROW?

$GROW is a symbolic, non-transferable token earned by staking $POI. It cannot be bought or sold and has no market value. $GROW is used to access a dedicated participation page where specific bounties may be voted on using $GROW voting weight. This provides emotional and reputational engagement without introducing financial risk or governance complexity.
